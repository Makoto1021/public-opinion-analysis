In this website, you can see public sentiment towards politicians in Japan's National Diet. You, as a politician, can monitor how people are reacting to your job on a daily basis.

Today's politics is not only about attending as many meetings as possible to make themselves known to the public. Politicians are required to adjust and improve their manifesto by analyzing people's reaction. This website provides the politicians with power to better understand public, by leveraging the technology of Natural Language Processing.

For this analysis, data are collected from tweets.
